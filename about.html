The Story Behind Codechype

Codechype was founded by a team of industry veterans who saw a gap between traditional computer science education and the practical skills needed in the tech industry. We noticed too many graduates knew theory but couldn't build a complete application.

Our solution is simple: a curriculum centered around project-based learning. Every student at Codechype builds a portfolio of real, impressive projects, ensuring they graduate not just with knowledge, but with tangible proof of their skills.
