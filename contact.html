Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our courses and services.

What programming languages do you teach?

We offer courses in Python, JavaScript, HTML/CSS, and more. Our curriculum covers both beginner and advanced levels across multiple programming languages and frameworks.

Do you provide placement assistance?

Yes! We offer comprehensive career support including resume building, interview preparation, mock interviews, and connections with our industry partners for job placements.

What is the duration of your courses?

Our courses range from 3 months for beginner programs to 6-12 months for advanced and full-stack development courses. We also offer flexible learning options and project-based learning.

Do you offer online classes?

Yes, we provide both online and offline classes. Our online platform includes live interactive sessions, recorded lectures, and 24/7 access to learning materials.

What are your class timings?

We offer flexible class timings including weekday evenings and weekend batches. Morning sessions are also available for working professionals. Contact us to discuss your preferred schedule.
