Absolutely not! This course is designed for complete beginners. We start from the very basics of design thinking and build up to professional-level skills. Many of our successful graduates had no prior design experience.

What tools will I learn?

You'll master Figma (industry-standard design tool), Miro (for collaborative brainstorming), and various usability testing tools. We also provide access to premium UI kits, icon libraries, and design resources worth thousands of dollars.

How long does it take to complete?

The course is designed to be completed in 8-10 weeks with 8-10 hours of study per week. However, you can go at your own pace. Many students take 12-14 weeks to fully absorb the concepts and complete projects.

While we can't guarantee employment, our graduates have a 94% job placement rate within 6 months. You'll build a professional portfolio, learn industry-standard tools, and get career guidance. Many graduates land roles at top companies like Google, Microsoft, and Adobe.

Everything! 200+ HD video lessons, premium design tools access, 50+ Figma templates, resource library, 1:1 mentor support, portfolio reviews, certificate of completion, and lifetime access to course materials.

Can I do this while working full-time?

Definitely! The course is designed for working professionals. All sessions are recorded, and you can study at your own schedule. Many of our students complete the course while working full-time jobs.
